Our Video

Sometimes, eating green is difficult in college. We made a quick cooking video on making vegan mac & cheese. Living vegan helps reduce the water consumption in America, spares animals from meat-industry related cruelty, and tastes great!

  • [Watch](https://youtu.be/fY2j-o6uysw) our Video!
    • **If you want to make our delicious vegan mac and cheese, follow the recipe and instructions below:** Ingredients 16 ounces of dry pasta 1/2 cup of raw cashews 1 tbsp olive oil 1/2 white onion 2 cloves garlic 2 carrots 1 serrano pepper 1/2 tsp cumin 1/2 tsp paprika 1 tsp chili powder 1 cup veggie stock 1/3 cup nutritional yeast 1 cup almond milk Instructions Off of the stove: Cover the raw cashews with boiling water and let them soak for one hour. Chop peel and chop the onion and garlic. Chop the pepper and carrots (peels optional). On the Stove: Coat a high-walled skillet with olive oil and cook the onion until translucent over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook for about three minutes. Add carrots and peppers to the pan and cook until peppers are slightly soft. Add the cumin, paprika, and chili powder and cook for three minutes. Add one cup of vegetable stock to the pan and allow it to simmer for 15 minutes over low heat. Bring a medium pot of water to a boil and add your dry pasta. Cook for about ten minutes or until desired consistency, and drain. Put the pasta back into the cooled pot. In a food processor: Drain the cashews and put them in a food processor. Add the almond milk and nutritional yeast. Pulse until combined. Add the vegetable mixture from the pan (including the juices) and blend until combined. Add more non-dairy milk until you reach the desired consistency. Add this ‘cheese’ mixture to the pasta and stir over low heat until combined. **Enjoy!**
